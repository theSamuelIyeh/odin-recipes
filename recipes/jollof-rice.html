Jollof Rice

Description

Jollof-rice is an african dish that is common among the nigerians. It is typically a mix of Rice and Tomato stew. It is preferably prepared with meat or fish.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Onions
  • Grinded Pepper
  • Spices
  • Water

Steps

  1. Cut and wash the beef
  2. Put in pot of water
  3. Add spices and allow to boil till soft
  4. Parboil rice
  5. Wash the rice in cold water
  6. Put rice in sift to drain water
  7. Put vegetable oil in another pot
  8. Pour in onions
  9. Pour in grinded pepper
  10. Allow to fry for 2 mins
  11. Add in tomato sauce
  12. Allow to fry for 2 mins
  13. Boil water in another pot
  14. Add spice in boiling water
  15. Add boiled meat water
  16. Add parboiled rice
  17. Add in fried mixture
  18. Allow to dry water off
  19. Fry beef in deep vegetable oil
  20. Serve rice and beef hot
