Beans

Description

Beans is very common food in africa especially nigeria. Beans is known to be an energy giver. in nigeria people love beans.

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Red Oil
  • Dry fish
  • Onions
  • Grinded Pepper
  • Spices
  • Water

Steps

  1. Pick and separate dirts from beans
  2. Add beans to pot of water and boil
  3. Immediately beans boil drain water and boil again
  4. Wash and add dry fish
  5. Once beans is soft, add red oil
  6. Also add onion and spices
  7. Add grinded pepper
  8. Allow for some minutes and serve hot
